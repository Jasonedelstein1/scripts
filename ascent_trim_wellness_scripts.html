Video Production Scripts

Production documentation for three essential video advertisements featuring Don's authentic weight loss journey and expertise. Each script is designed to resonate with prospective patients through storytelling, education, and genuine connection.

Script 1: Manitou Incline

Concept: Visual metaphor using weighted backpack to represent the burden of obesity
Estimated Runtime: 60-90 seconds
Primary Message: Weight loss isn't about willpower—it's about getting help to start your journey

EXTERIOR: Parking lot at base of Manitou Incline. Don stands with a heavily loaded backpack.

"In 1998 I was 400lbs, and today I'm going back."

"I've filled this backpack with the heaviest objects I could find in my house, and we're gonna climb the Manitou Incline."

Don steps on scale with backpack, adjusting items.

"Almost there, I don't think I can really fit anything else in this backpack so I'll say that's close enough."

B-Roll: Close-up of scale display showing weight, items visible in backpack

Cut to Don at base of incline with backpack.

"Okay so for those that don't know, the Manitou Incline is basically this staircase that goes straight up a mountain in Manitou Springs."

"I actually run this pretty often, my PR is 45 minutes, I think it's gonna take a bit longer today."

B-Roll: Wide shot of incline, establishing shot showing the steep stairs

Don climbing the stairs, struggling with weighted backpack. Various angles showing effort, breathing heavily.

"It is crazy to think that I used to carry this much weight around."

Don stops, drops backpack with a thud.

"I don't think it's going to happen today."

"The key to a healthy lifestyle is obviously diet & exercise, but it can be so hard to get to a place where you can even begin to consider diet & exercise when every step takes this much effort."

B-Roll: Close-up of backpack hitting ground, Don catching breath

Don speaking to camera, more composed.

"At Ascent Trim & Wellness we've helped so many people get back to who they are— who they want to be."

"You don't need to carry this with you for the rest of your life."

Don tries to lift backpack, struggles.

B-Roll: Consider footage of Don without backpack, walking freely up stairs or at summit

Don at base or on incline, confident stance.

"If you're ready to be you again, contact us today to start your wellness journey."

B-Roll: Ascent Trim & Wellness logo, contact information overlay

Script 2: Personal Story

Concept: Don's authentic weight loss journey and motivation for starting the clinic
Estimated Runtime: 60-75 seconds
Primary Message: Building trust through vulnerability and establishing credibility

Don speaking directly to camera.

"If these drugs existed when I was overweight— I would've saved myself from 2 near-death experiences during surgery, 10s of thousands of dollars, and probably at least an extra 5 years of life."

Historic photos of Don at various stages appear on screen.

"Back when I went through my weight-loss journey every strategy was either invasive & dangerous, or just downright ridiculous."

B-Roll: Scan through provided historic photos, showing progression

Continue with photos or Don speaking to camera.

"I was 8 years old when I tried Jenny Craig. I must've mis-interpreted something because I ended up eating half my weight in carrots everyday. After all that, I didn't lose any weight and I still have to wear glasses."

Note: Light moment—keep tone warm despite difficult subject

Don speaking directly, serious tone.

"And then much later, after getting discharged from the military for being overweight, I tried gastric bypass and almost died."

Don speaking with conviction.

"I opened Ascent Trim & Wellness because it's the kind of weight loss clinic that I wish was there for me when I was going through my wellness journey."

Footage of Don being active (running, hiking, exercising).

"There is so much shame surrounding obesity, I want every patient to know that we see you for who you are."

"And we're here to help you get back to you again."

B-Roll: Capture multiple activities showing healthy, active lifestyle

Return to Don speaking to camera or overlay on active footage.

"Click 'Book Now' to schedule your consultation with us today."

B-Roll: Ascent logo, booking button/link overlay

Script 3: Educational

Concept: Science-based explanation of how modern weight loss treatments work
Estimated Runtime: 60-75 seconds
Primary Message: Educating viewers on the biological basis of hunger and modern solutions
Production Note: This script relies heavily on A-Roll of Don explaining, supplemented with animations/stock footage to illustrate concepts

Don speaking to camera.

"Here's the problem with traditional weight loss: it treats hunger like it's optional."

"Just eat less. Just move more. Just have more self-control."

"But hunger isn't a choice— it's a chemical signal demanding attention."

Animation or diagram showing brain and digestive system.

"So we eat. And if everything is working correctly our brain gets a message that we're full, and it's time to stop eating."

Animation: Simple graphic showing eating → signal → brain

Animation showing hormone pathway and degradation.

"That message comes from a hormone called GLP-1, but the problem is it has a half-life of 2 minutes, meaning that for so many people that signal is almost entirely degraded by the time it reaches your brain, meaning you're going to keep eating."

Animation: Show GLP-1 hormone fading/degrading before reaching brain receptor

Animation showing treatment activating brain receptor.

"Modern weight loss treatments work by activating the same receptor in your brain that GLP-1 activates."

"Rather than trying to shrink the stomach or the esophagus, they address the root of the problem— the brain that thinks it still needs more food."

Animation: Highlight brain receptor activation, show contrast with surgical approaches

Continue animation or return to Don.

"Your pancreas responds. Your brain responds. And for the first time, the hunger quiets."

"This isn't about restriction or surgery. It's about giving your brain the information it's been missing."

Don speaking to camera with confidence.

"Book your consultation now and discover what sustainable weight loss actually feels like."

B-Roll: Ascent logo, booking button/link overlay
