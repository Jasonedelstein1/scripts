Script 3: Qualification Message - "Not For Everybody"

Duration: 60 seconds

Type: Direct-to-camera educational/qualification

Talent: Sloan Stiles

Primary CTA: Book free consultation

Tone: Honest, professional, health-focused

Location: Salon

If you want thicker, fuller hair that blends in seamlessly with your existing hair, hair extensions might be for you, but they're not for everybody.
There are too many unlicensed and unqualified stylists that will install hair extensions on anyone who walks through their door.
That's not me.
When you install hair extensions, you are putting weight on your existing hair follicles. When done properly, this weight can be balanced and can even lead to hair health benefits by reducing the impact of heat and products on your real hair, but when done improperly they can pull at those follicles and lead to more hair loss, which is the opposite of what you want when you're looking for hair extensions.
That's why I focus my hair extension consultations on existing hair health.
Everyone's hair is different, and there is no one-size-fits-all solution when it comes to hair extensions.
