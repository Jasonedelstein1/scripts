Type: Direct-to-camera educational/qualification

That's why I focus my hair extension consultations on existing hair health.

Production Notes

  • Consider educational graphics showing proper vs improper extension placement
  • Serious, professional tone throughout - this is about trust and expertise
  • B-roll could include Sloan examining client hair during consultation
  • Emphasize the word "free" in the final CTA
  • End with clear booking information and contact details

General Production Guidelines

Branding & Visual Consistency

  • All videos should maintain professional lighting that showcases hair quality
  • Sloan should be professionally styled in all direct-to-camera content
  • Consistent color grading across all three videos
  • Include Sloan's logo and contact information in end cards
  • Use clean, professional backgrounds that don't distract from the message

Audio Requirements

  • Lavalier microphone for all talent
  • Clean audio with minimal background noise
  • Consider subtle background music for testimonial video
  • Professional audio mixing and mastering

Call-to-Action Elements

  • Primary CTA: "Book Now" button linking to consultation scheduler
  • Contact Information: Display phone number, website, or booking link
  • End Cards: 5-7 seconds with contact details and social media handles
  • On-Screen Text: Reinforce key points and credentials

Platform Optimization

  • Vertical Format (9:16): Primary delivery for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook/Instagram Stories
  • Square Format (1:1): Secondary delivery for Instagram Feed
  • Horizontal Format (16:9): YouTube, website embedding
  • Captions: All videos should include burned-in captions for accessibility and sound-off viewing
