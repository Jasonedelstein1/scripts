Script 1

If you're looking to get hair extensions done in Denver, here are 3 helpful things to consider

#1 They require maintenance just like your natural hair, but probably not as much as you think, typically an average of an hour and half in the salon every 6 weeks

#2 They can damage your natural hair if done improperly, this is why I've been certified by 5 different companies in different installation methods to learn as much as possible about how and why to install in very specific ways that prioritize the health of your hair above all

#3 Similarly to affecting the health of your natural hair, if done improperly they can also look obvious, one of my favorite things is when my clients come back and say "I've been getting compliments but no one seems to know I have extensions in!" They almost want people to know, but for me this is a huge compliment, that they are undetectable and natural looking is the goal

My name is Sloan and I'd love to assist you on this journey if you've been considering getting extensions, lets make sure they're the right fit for both your budget and lifestyle, there should be a book now button below to get started by booking a complimentary consultation.
